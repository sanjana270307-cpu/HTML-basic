The Best movies according to Sanjana

My top 3 movies

1. Nanban

Nanban is a Tamil-language coming-of-age comedy-drama film directed by Shankar.
The film features an ensemble cast including Vijay, Jiiva, and Srikanth.
It tells the story of three friends who embark on a journey to find their true selves while navigating the challenges of college life.

2. Golmaal again

Golmaal Again is a Hindi-language comedy film directed by Rohit Shetty.
It is the fourth installment in the Golmaal film series and features an ensemble cast including Ajay Devgn, Arshad Warsi, and Tusshar Kapoor.
The film follows a group of friends who return to their childhood home and encounter supernatural events.

3. 83

83 is a Hindi-language sports drama film directed by Kabir Khan.
The film is based on the Indian cricket team's victory in the 1983 Cricket World Cup and features Ranveer Singh as Kapil Dev.
It showcases the journey of the underdog Indian team as they strive to win the prestigious tournament.
